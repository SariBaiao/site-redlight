Beamom

The birth of a baby can trigger a jumble of powerful emotions, from excitement and joy to fear and anxiety. But it can also result in something you might not expect — depression.
- *Mayo Clinic*

BeAMom is a product designed to help recent moms overcome postpartum depression.

Indentity and Design

WEB DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT

In a project contracted by the University of Coimbra, BeAMom is a web app that uses a mixed approach of long-reads, exercises and mindfulness to fight mood swings, anxiety and difficulty in having a good night of sleep that last for a long time.

BeAMom is designed to provide a calm and relaxing experience, so that moms can start enjoying life and loving their children with a healthy mind.
